Design a full-page, futuristic and professional landing page for a private invite-only cyber team called "HUSTLER TEAM". The theme is neon pink and black, with glitch effects, cyberpunk aesthetics, and a high-tech feel. The website is styled for dark mode.

📌 GENERAL STYLE:
- Neon pink and black/dark grey as main color scheme
- Glowing UI elements and soft shadows
- Futuristic fonts and layouts
- High-tech, digital, and encrypted feel
- Subtle glitch art effects (on borders, titles, hover buttons)
- Realistic lighting and subtle movement suggested (for animations)
- Cyber grid background or matrix lines with pink pulses
